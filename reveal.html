01
Challenge 01

Launch Smitten
in Malta.

Can we replicate the Iceland magic in a brand new market? Malta is strikingly similar — 570k population, island nation, tight-knit social scene, flooded with tourists. The challenge: make Smitten the dating app for Maltese locals and make it actually stick.

Constraint Near-zero marketing budget. No paid ads, no boosted posts, no shortcuts. Pure hustle, community, and word of mouth only.
02
Challenge 02

Organic
Growth Hacks.

What can we build outside of Smitten to drive organic growth, increase runway, and boost revenue? Viral tools, content plays, communities, utilities — anything that funnels people our way without living inside the app.

Constraint Cannot be a feature or anything built within Smitten itself. It must stand completely on its own two feet.
03
Challenge 03

AI Dating
Profile Agent.

Build a standalone AI agent that creates the best, most compelling dating profiles on the internet. Works across Tinder, Hinge, Bumble — its own product entirely. Not a Smitten feature.

Constraint Radical authenticity. The AI can only work with real facts the user provides — no fabrication, no fake personas. The hard part is making the truth irresistible.
